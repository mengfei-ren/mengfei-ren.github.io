I am a Ph.D Candidate in the [Computer Science and Engineering Department](https://cse.uta.edu/) at [UT Arlington](http://www.uta.edu/uta/). I received my master degree from [](https://ist.psu.edu/)[UT Arlington](http://www.uta.edu/uta/) and bachelor degree from [China University of Petroleum](https://www.cup.edu.cn/). My research interests span Software Engineering and IoT Security. I am endeavoring to apply traditional software engineering techniques, such as fuzz testing, taint analysis, to IoT environment in order to help people find software vulnerabilities. Currently, I work as a graduate research assistant with [Dr. Jeff Lei](https://mentis.uta.edu/explore/profile/yu-lei) in UTA Software Engineering Research Center (SERC). I'm also co-supervised by [Dr. Jiang Ming](https://ranger.uta.edu/~ming/).
