About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Alabama in Huntsville. I received my PhD from The University of Texas at Arlington. My research interests span Software Engineering and IoT Security. I am endeavoring to develop robust and integrative security testing tools combining advanced software engineering techniques, such as fuzz testing and data flow analysis, for the security analysis of IoT wireless network protocols.

I'm always looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students who would like to carry on top-notch research on Software and IoT Security. Candidates are expected to have a solid background in software engineering, software testing, operating systems, IoT security, and strong programming skills. Please contact me if you are interested.
