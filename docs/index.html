About Me

I am a Ph.D candidate of Computer Science in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at UT Arlington, co-supervised by Dr. Jeff Lei and Dr. Jiang Ming. I received my Master from UT Arlington and Bachelor from China University of Petroleum. Prior to starting my PhD program at UT Arlington, I also worked in industry for several years as a software engineer, focusing on front-end development.

My research interests span Software Engineering and IoT Security. I am endeavoring to apply software techniques, such as fuzz testing, taint analysis, and symbolic execution, to help people find security vulnerabilities in IoT devices.
