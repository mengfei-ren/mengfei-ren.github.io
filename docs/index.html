About Me

I am a Ph.D candidate of Computer Science in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at UT Arlington, co-supervised by Dr. Yu (Jeff) Lei and Dr. Jiang Ming. I received my Master from UT Arlington and Bachelor from China University of Petroleum. Prior to starting my PhD program at UT Arlington, I also worked in the industry for several years as a software engineer, focusing on front-end development.

My research interests span Software Engineering and IoT Security. I am endeavoring to develop robust and integrative security testing tools that apply advanced software engineering techniques such as fuzz testing and data flow analysis to the security analysis of IoT wireless network protocols.
